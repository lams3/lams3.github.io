Path Tracer

A Monte Carlo based Path Tracer, featuring a custom scene and material description, allowing Photorealistic renderization of .OBJ files. It was my first atempt on global illumintation. Materials have Diffuse, Specular, Transmissive, and Refraction coefficients, alowing for flexibility on their definition. Diffuse, Normal and Displacement Textures may be applied for enhanced details.

  • Fully written in Modern C++
  • Multithread for best efficience

Voxar Renderer

A real-time renderer made as a job application for Voxar Labs. It features Diffuse, Specular, Reflection and Normal Texture Mapping, a FPS-style camera with the ability to experiment Dolly-Zoom effect. The Reflection Mapping may be static or dynamic(re-rendering an cube-map every frame for the object).

  • C++
  • OpenGL
